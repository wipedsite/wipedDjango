FAQs

Who can register as a Wiper?

Who can register a Wiper? Anyone who takes pride in their work and has attention to detail. It is not neccessary for you to have a car but you will need an Iphone!

We’re already looking for Wipers in many areas now, and with other locations soon to launch, watch out for updates! See where we operate on our homepage. Currently we are focusing on the Somerset West and Stellenbosch area.

How can I register to join Wiped as a Wiper?

Fill in the form above to start the registration process.

Once verified, we’ll contact you with the tools required to start your Wiper journey.

Do I need special training to work with Wiped?

Every Wiper who joins Wiped needs to deliver exceptional service for our customers. Our waterless technology makes the job very easy but we will send you a video tutorial once you complete the verification process.

Our local teams occassionally hold training sessions to ensure Wipers are fully equipped with all information and materials needed for a smooth experience. Any livery needed for your area can be collected from your local office.

How do I check the status of my application?

We’ll email you with a status update on your application. The verification process usually takes 48 hours from receipt of all correct documentation.

How much does it cost to sign up?

Registration is absolutely free! All you need is the required documents and an iOS smartphone with an active data connection to join Wiped.
